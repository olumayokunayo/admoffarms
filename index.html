About Us

Quality Poultry Farming in Nigeria

Admof Farms is a purpose-driven livestock business based in Ibadan, Nigeria. We operate as a full-cycle poultry farm specializing in raising broiler breeder chickens to produce strong, fast-growing broiler chicks for farmers across the country.

We also grow out our own birds for hygienically processed frozen chicken, and fatten rams and goats to meet local meat demand.

Our Mission & Vision

Mission

To raise and deliver high-quality livestock that feed families, support farmers, and contribute to food security in Nigeria and beyond.

Vision

To become one of Africa's leading agribusinesses in poultry and meat production — known for innovation, consistency, and impact.
